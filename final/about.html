    About the Artist

    Katherine "Katie" Browne has been independently learning and practicing different art techniques with different mediums throughout her life.
    From an early age, Katie's mom had her taking various painting and drawing classes. Though it took several years for her to get into drawing, she took a liking to art almost immediately.

    Katie is now continuing her informal artistic studies in her free time while she studies psychology at the University of Montana.

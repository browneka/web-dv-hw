About Jamie Mortara

Jamie Mortara is a young poet from New Jersey. They have earned a Masters of Fine Arts degree in creative writing from the University of North Carolina Wilmington. They compete in slam poetry competitions all over the United States and even hold the title of "co-slammaster" of Slamlandia, which is a slma poetry event that takes place in Portland, Oregon. Outside of competing, they have authored multiple chapbooks. Mortara's work has been published in various journals and on several websites.

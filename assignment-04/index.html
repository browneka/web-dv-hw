When You Ask Me How I Got This Way

Jamie Mortara

i want to say well it’s kind of like that scene in Disney’s Alice In Wonderland the one where the Hatter grabs the giant pocketwatch from the panicking White Rabbit how he declares it Two Days Slow and then he dips it right into his coffee


and then he throws it against the table and then he pours salt into its springs then he forks out its gears he slathers it in butter pours tea into it spreads jam over it squeezes some lemon juice to finish it like some kind of sickly oyster and slams it shut


i want to ask if you remember that but then i want to say when i was born something in my head wasn’t quite right


but then i want to say when i was a kid something in my school wasn’t quite right


but then i want to say when i loved this person something in our house wasn’t quite right


so instead i say who can really tell what exactly broke me?


because on top of all of that when the Hatter shuts the watch All Done (as if he somehow fixed it) it suddenly spins and sputters and freaks out all over the dinner table and they grab this giant fucking hammer and crush the clock to a halt anyway which is to say: i don’t know which damage to blame here was that last toxic partner the Coffee or the Butter or the Hammer? which one were you? the insult? or the injury? does it matter?


do you think the Hammer might have whispered to the Watch oh you’re already telling this all wrong anyway?


do you think it might have grumbled you were already fucked up when i found you?


do you think the Watch apologized to the Hammer like i did?


do you think it said no no you’re right i’m sorry you’re right i’m just Two Days Slow
